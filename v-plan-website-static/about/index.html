Introducing Our New Construction Division

From estimating costs on your next project to finishing it up, V Plan has helped hundreds of construction companies boost profits and reduce unnecessary costs using simple but effective organizational-technology solutions.

We strive to build a relationship with our clients and help them navigate the confusing and sometimes costly process of estimating, bidding and executing construction projects.

We offer top-tier, end-to-end solutions for creating profitable construction businesses for the following contractors:

Trade Contractors
Project Owners
General Contractors

Our on-demand project-estimating services and custom construction-management software, Realtraker™, combine to create a system where you will always be ahead of any problems you may encounter at any point in your project journey. Not only do we provide the software solution, we even take care of setting up and managing clients’ projects through Realtraker™.

Quantity Takeoff and Estimating Services

Using the advanced software tools combined with decades of experience, V Plan helps clients with on-demand estimating services, saving time and money.

We look at everything from the pre-construction phase and complete a full detailed measurement of materials and labour needed to finish the project.

From there we make suggestions on where you can save money and cut unnecessary costs in order to improve your end of project profit. We are always aware of issues that can pop up throughout any construction project so we help you make adjustments on the fly.

Our team has worked with hundreds of other construction companies so you can be confident our data and advice is accurate and always puts your company top of mind.

Our Origins

With over 30 years in the construction industry, our founder and the creator of Realtraker™, Raj Kachu, has seen it all.

Raj has successfully managed numerous projects from bidding to delivery across the Middle East, Asia, and North America. His experience spans multiple segments of construction markets.

In 2015, Raj founded V Plan because he wanted to use his experience and knowledge to help other companies improve their workflow and management throughout each new construction project.

He created the Realtraker™ software to go alongside V Plan because he felt companies needed a better way to manage everything involved with the day-to-day tasks of overseeing construction projects. He pulled from his extensive experience and created a program that helped develop solutions to the most common problems he ran into when managing both large and small projects.
